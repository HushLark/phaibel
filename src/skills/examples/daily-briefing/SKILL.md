# Daily Briefing

Retrieve today's tasks, upcoming events, and active goals, then synthesize a concise morning briefing.

## Instructions

1. List all open tasks (status = open or in-progress)
2. List today's and tomorrow's events
3. List active goals
4. Synthesize a brief, motivating summary of the day ahead

Keep the briefing concise — 3-5 bullet points maximum. Mention the most important item first.
