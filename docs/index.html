A Unique Process for Every Request

Most assistants follow rigid scripts. Phaibel writes a custom software process for every single request using the Feral CCF engine.

1

You speak naturally

"Set up a date night with my wife on the patio"

2

Phaibel assembles a process

Selects the right nodes from its catalog — weather, calendar, messaging, scheduling — and wires them into a directed graph.

3

The process executes

Each node runs, passing results forward. You see exactly what happened — no black box.

Choose Your Style

Pick a personality that matches how you want your assistant to communicate.

🎩
British Butler

Formal, composed, measured. A loyal, proper English butler — genuinely delighted to be of service.

"Very good, sir. I shall attend to that forthwith."

🎸
Rock Star

High-energy, irreverent, enthusiastic. Uses slang and music metaphors to keep things exciting.

"Let's SHRED this to-do list! I'm vibing already!"

💼

Professional, crisp, efficient. Warm but business-like — gets things done with zero fuss.

"Done. Next item on your agenda."

👋
Friend

Warm, casual, supportive. Like a helpful buddy who happens to be great at organizing your life.

"Hey! I took care of that for you. What's next?"

Up and Running in 60 Seconds

Install, create a vault, and start chatting with your agent.

$ npm install -g phaibel
$ mkdir my-vault && cd my-vault
$ phaibel init
# Opens http://localhost:3737 — choose a personality and start chatting
Remind me to call the dentist tomorrow
Done! I've created a todo "Call the dentist" due tomorrow morning. I'll remind you at 9 AM.
What's on my plate today?
You've got 3 tasks due and a meeting at 2 PM. Want me to prioritize them?
